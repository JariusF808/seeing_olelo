Point the camera and objects will be labeled in ʻŌlelo Hawaiʻi with English translation.

Settings are saved in your browser.

About Our App

The purpose of Seeing ʻŌlelo is to use your camera to recognize items and display their names in ʻŌlelo Hawaiʻi, with English translations.

This demo uses an on-device AI model (COCO-SSD). Add more translations in the code’s hawMap section, found in the JS section of our code.
